Who We Are

Hands-on Math was founded by two teachers frustrated with traditional math methods. We built this platform to make math engaging, meaningful, and rooted in real understanding.

What We Offer

Math Games 🎮

Interactive games and puzzles that build essential math skills through play, addressing a whole range of topics and skills related to primary Math.

Exploratory Activities 🔍

Hands-on activities that enable kids to deeply engage with concepts, and discover patterns in numbers.

Guiding Math at Home 🏠

Thoughtful articles and advice on helping you guide your child's math journey, addressing common struggles.

Creative Kits 🎨

Comprehensive kits that bring math to life at home or in the classroom.

In-Person Classes

We offer hands-on math classes in select locations (in Bangalore), based on demand and community interest. If you think your area would be a great fit, get in touch with us to explore bringing our classes to your community.
